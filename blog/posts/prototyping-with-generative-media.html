Toolkit · January 2024

Prototyping with Generative Media

A starter toolkit for prototyping with generative visuals, audio, and text so creative teams can iterate at the speed of thought.

Layer generative assets with manual edits

Generative media thrives when it is directionally correct. I use AI outputs as first drafts, then layer manual edits to lock tone, typography, and pacing. This keeps the work personal while speeding up exploration.

Build a reusable asset library

Every experiment feeds a shared library of prompts, color palettes, and motion studies. Tagging assets by mood and interaction pattern saves time when a brief calls for similar energy later on.

Prototype the narrative, not just the visuals

Storyboards, voiceover scripts, and lightweight interface prototypes all benefit from generative acceleration. I create parallel tracks—text, audio, motion—to stress test how a story feels when assembled.
